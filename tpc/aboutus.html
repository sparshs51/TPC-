The Cell

The Placement Office is responsible for campus placement at IET Lucknow. The Placement-in- Charge, the Assistant Placement Officer, the Placement Office staff and the student representatives handle various crucial tasks in the office like reaching out to companies, scheduling & executing placement related activities and carrying out all official communication with recruiters and with students. The team strives to strike a match between recruiter expectations and student aspirations. The team of student representatives consists of the Placement Managers who coordinate the activities of other student members and ensure compliance with various institute policies; Company Coordinators who execute coordination with the recruiting organizations; and Department Placement Coordinators who steer student preparedness and employability enhancement initiatives. The Placement Office is well-equipped with excellent infrastructure to support every stage of the placement process.

    Facilities

      A state of the art workspace for all official proceedings of the placement cell.
  • Interview Room
      • 6 Air conditioned interview cabins for the visiting companies for the personal interview or panel rounds. Rooms connected with high-speed internet on WiFi enabled PCs with printing facilities.
  • Group Discussion Room
      • A dedicated Group Discussion Hall for placement procedure rounds by visiting companies on campus.
  • Computer Center
      • At immediate disposal of the recruiters, labs of all departments shall be available with modern and adequate facilities to conduct the technical rounds in a hassle-free and smooth manner.
  • Presentation Room
      • The presentation room is a well-connected, air conditioned lecture theatre with projection system, sound system, live conferencing facility and a seating capacity of about 120 candidates.
  • Auditorium
      • A newly constructed state of art auditorium with a seating capacity of 850 for Pre-Placement Talks, Seminars and Demonstrations.
  • Training Classroom
      • A separate training centre for students of all departments with necessary facilities and equipments, to make the students technically sound and professionally ready.
  • Other Facilites
      • Provision of lodging and transport facilities for the visiting HRs Along-with choice of cuisine for the meals.
